Executive Summary

The fiscal period concluded with a total revenue of $29.4M, representing a strong 25.56% growth compared to the previous year. Our customer base has expanded to 18,482 total users, supported by a healthy influx of 931 new acquisitions this quarter.

Critical Alert: Despite revenue growth, 75% of our customer base is currently exhibiting high-risk churn behavior. Immediate intervention is required for the 13,857 customers in the "Churn Risk" category to protect the projected $1.61M monthly run-rate.
